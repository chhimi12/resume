Everest Trek

Mount Everest, the tallest peak in the world, attracts adventurers and trekkers to Nepal's Solukhumbu District. It's protected by Sagarmatha National Park, established in 1976, covering 1,148 square kilometers and featuring elevations from 2,845 to 8,848 meters. The region offers breathtaking views of peaks like Lhotse and Makalu, alongside rich cultural experiences with local communities, including Sherpas and Tamangs. TrekNepal360 provides tailored trekking itineraries, from teahouse to camping treks, ensuring memorable adventures through routes like the Everest Base Camp Trek and Gokyo-Chola Pass Trek. Our experienced team is dedicated to meeting your trekking needs and preferences.
